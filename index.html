Proven In The Pilot

It's already working.

Across two pilot sessions at Evans High School in Spring 2026, students engaged more deeply with nutrition science when it was delivered through spatial computing and heritage-rooted recipes — and the technology proved genuinely accessible, not exclusive.

The pilot reached exactly the population most affected by diet-related chronic disease, making this a measurable equity intervention with real, documented demand for more.
